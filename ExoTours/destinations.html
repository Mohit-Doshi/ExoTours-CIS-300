Space Tourism Destinations

A majority of space tourism, contrary to popular belief, happens on Earth itself! Most missions' durations won't spend more than 6 minutes in space. The ones based at the ISS were in LEO (Low-Earth Orbit). There are plans to go around (SpaceX) or on the moon. Many companies have set their eyes on Mars and the Asteroid Belt.

Destination Type Travel Duration (Expected) Distance Cost per Ticket
